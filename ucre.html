Executive Summary

Home&Home (H&H) is an online community for home buyers. Home&Home is filled with experts, such as real estate agents and loan experts, and other home-buyers. Users can find groups that match their needs, preferences, and/or backgrounds for more personalized communication and help. Through H&H, we build a strong home buyer community.

Problem & HMW Statement

Young adults are the next generation home buyers. How might we better support young adults in their home-buying journey through social interactions?

Methods

To better understand young adults’ perceptions and attitudes about home buying, we conducted a series of user research activities. We used background research and think-aloud activity to find the problem space. We then used contextual interview and affinity diagramming to target user needs. Then, we did multiple storyboards, speed dating, and experience prototyping to explore possible solutions and decide our final solution.

Evidence (Quotes)

"For important things like home buying, I would like to reach out to experts for suggestions."

"I need to talk with real people, instead of just searching information online."

"It would be interesting to be connected with people with similar preferences, such as those who also like sunshine."

Insights

1. To better understand young adults’ perceptions and attitudes about home buying, we conducted a series of user research activities. We used background research and think-aloud activity to find the problem space. We then used contextual interview and affinity diagramming to target user needs. Then, we did multiple storyboards, speed dating, and experience prototyping to explore possible solutions and decide our final solution.

2. Young adults prefer interacting with real people for home buying.

3. Young adults need more basic education on financial literacy related to home-buying.

4. Young adults prefer consolidated and personalized guidance and information when buying a home.
