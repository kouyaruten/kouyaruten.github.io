Golf Desk

Reddot Award 2018: Winner

Product Design/ Group work(2 designers)/ Teammate: Wei Xinli(Shirley Wei)
My role: Leader, Ideation, Form, Modelling, Rendering

Project Brief

Golf Desk is a desk allowing users to clean it up easily - just like playing golf. There's nothing simpler than that - sweep the surface of the desk by hands, and those clutters will be swept into the hole on the desk. Golf Desk also conveys its concept to users - design should be harmonious with our life. A good design should make life more convenient and easier, instead of bringing new problems. The form of Golf Desk is also an application of Affordance - a visual cue to indicate the proper way for a user to interact with an object (by Donald A. Norman). The elegant hole on the desk could indicate to users that they should put something into it.

Background Research: Anxiety & Desk

According to a report released by University of Michigan, people staying in the office are prone to irritability. The clutters and files on the desk will produce a bad feeling in people's mind, which is also a cause of some relevant diseases, according to a latest research. The research also points out that cleaning up the desk is an effective way to relieve anxiety and stress.

67% of the people are living a sedentary lifestyle.
40% of the people often feel irritable because of the paper, files and other clutters on the desk.
83% of the people have seen colleagues get mad at the office.
63% of the people have got mad at the office.

Inspiration

The color and shape of the hole on the desk is inspired by golf, in order to encourage people to release their stress when tidying the desk - just like playing golf.

Design Concept: Play Golf on Your Desk!

Golf Desk is a desk allowing users to clean it up easily - just like playing golf. There's nothing simpler than that - sweep the surface of the desk by hands, and those clutters will be swept into the hole on the desk. Life should be as simple as this desk, which has both functional and aesthetic value. Golf Desk also conveys its concept to users - design should be harmonious with our life. A good design should make life more convenient and easier, instead of bringing new problems.
