Design Process

Instead of the design methods, designers should focus on the logic of their design in a whole design process. Design Bridge is a method ("created" by myself) to connect designers' research and their design. It's a better way to directly express the findings in the research and how these findings could shape the design. As the name suggests, it's like a bridge, connecting the research and the design in a whole process.
