IBM Design Studio

Time: Feb - Jun 2018 . Team: Design Studio . Role: Product Design Intern

Brief

I interned at the IBM Design Studio in 2018, during which I worked on several to-B and to-C design projects.
Tools: Sketch, Invision, Principle, Html, Imovie, ...

EDPA Data Management Platform

EDPA is a platform used by engineers helping companies manage their big data. As the only designer in my team, I redesigned its information architecture and interfaces with several engineers in a week.

Before Starting

My knowledge in SQL and statistics made it easier to understand this product. However, I still had to learn a lot.

The most important 3 concepts in EDPA are category, term and data set. A category contains 0 or more terms, which can also be grouped by data sets.

Information Architecture

My design focused on the Data Asset Management tab. The 3 major needs for users are:
Check and manipulate the Asset Index;
Manage the Data Set;
Check Data Quality.

Since they are not closely related, I decided to put them in a hamburger navigation as three major tabs in Data Asset Management.

Asset Index

The tree view clearly shows the hierarchy of categories and terms, allowing users to find a category/term manually. There's also a global search bar to help them search by keywords or status. Because of the limited space, I designed a navigation drawer (Event tab) to show notifications when the data updates.

Data Set Management

It took me a while to persuade engineers in my team to implement the Card Based Design, by Competitor Analysis and A/B Testing. From my opinion, when there's only a few of the elements and we don't have to sort them, Cards are better than tables.

Turning on / off the automatic update is important. Therefore, the according button was designed blue and other buttons are icons from IBM design language and are greyscale.

What did I learn from that week?

- Only when we understand the technical and business part of the product can we use design to drive it.
- Product and Design Thinking are important even for engineers.
- It's important to learn how to communicate with and persuade others.

RPA

RPA is a robot management platform. I designed several interfaces collaborating with senior designers and made a hi-fi prototype by using Invison and principle.

I made several simple prototypes to test the interaction process of the ROI Calculator and found slider, rather than text field, scrollbar or pie chart, was a better choice.

EMMA

I redesigned the interaction process and interfaces of EMMA, which is a web-based platform allowing companies to asses their positions.

Smart Community
